How to Play

Goal: Defeat all enemy teams by destroying their capitals and units.

Controls:
Left Click Select units, move, attack, interact with UI
Right Click Cancel selection / placement mode
ESC Open menu / Cancel
Enter End turn
F11 Toggle fullscreen

Tips: Click on your capital to spawn units. Use the Create menu to build new capitals, hospitals, or spawn seers.
